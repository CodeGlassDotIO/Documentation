# Account

Your CodeGlass account is also used for licensing and billing. Licensing and billing are provided through our payment provider ([Stripe](https://stripe.com/)) and license provider([Licensespring](https://licensespring.com/)).

Their services and policies also apply:

- [LicenseSpring](https://licensespring.com/terms-of-service)
- [Stripe](https://stripe.com/customer-portal/legal)

## LicenseSpring CodeGlass Account

After purchasing or being assigned a license, you receive an email from LicenseSpring containing your account credentials.

This account allows you to:
- Log in to the CodeGlass client.
- Activate or deactivate devices.
- Manage assigned licenses.
- Change your password

More details can be found in the [License Portal](account/license-portal) documentation.

If you are a [license manager](account/license-portal#license-manager), you can also create accounts for others.

## Stripe CodeGlass Account

If you paid for your subscription, you will receive access to the **Stripe Customer Portal**. This is used to:
- Manage billing information and view invoices.
- Cancel or update your subscription.

Your Stripe account uses the same email address as your [LicenseSpring CodeGlass account](#licensespring-codeglass-account).

You can learn more about managing your billing and subscriptions on the [Change Subscription](./account/change-subscription) page.
